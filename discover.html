Discover UMD

Why I Chose UMD

My decision to attend UMD was influenced by the positive experiences of my family and friends who graduated from here. Their enthusiastic recommendations and fond memories of their time at UMD played a significant role in my choice.

Another major factor that drew me to UMD was its unique location. The campus provides quick and easy access to outdoor activities, which aligns perfectly with my passion for nature and outdoor recreation. This balance of quality education and outdoor opportunities made UMD the ideal choice for my college experience.

What I Love About Duluth

One of the most captivating aspects of Duluth is its stunning location alongside Lake Superior. The views from campus looking down the hill to the vast expanse of the lake never cease to amaze me, offering a unique and inspiring backdrop to daily student life.

What makes Duluth truly special is its distinct four-season experience, something that sets it apart from many other college towns across the country. Each season brings its own character and beauty to the city, from the vibrant fall colors and snowy winters to the fresh spring renewals and warm summer days by the lake.
